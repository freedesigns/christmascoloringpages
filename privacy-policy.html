5. CCPA Privacy Rights (For California Residents)

Under the CCPA, California residents have the right to:

  • Request the collection and use of their personal data.
  • Request that personal data be deleted.
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data).

If you make a request, we have one month to respond. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@christmascoloringpages.com.

  • Restrict processing of your personal data or object to how we use your data.
  • Request data portability for the transfer of your data.

If you have any concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us
